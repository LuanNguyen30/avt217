Design Brief and Site Content

Brief

I am a young artist looking for a place to share my artistic creations. With a portfolio, I am able to advertise myself and showcase my progress as a student and my level of understanding to my craft. I mainly specializes in digital art, but my portfolio extends outside of digital media and into physical medias such as sculptures, drawings, and paintings. Since I am a BFA major, I think it's best for me to have a dedicated portfolio webpage to act as a blog and why certain works exsist.

The intent of this portfolio site is to showcase my skillset for potential employers and curious onlookers. The goal of this website is to have a simple and modern aesthetic that is easily approchable and easy to navigate. The quality of my art should reflect the quality of my website.
