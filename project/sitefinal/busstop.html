Bus Stop

Digital Art 54 x 32 inches

The idea of "Bus Stop" came to me when I was reminiscing about the good ol' days when I was elementary school. Back when I waited in the morning at the bus stop for another day of elementary. Things were more peaceful those days and life was chill, so I made this painting to capture that exact feeling of waiting at the stop, feeling the breeze, and being truly at peace.
